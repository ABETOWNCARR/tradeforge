# Privacy Policy for TradeForge

_Last updated: July 9, 2026_

TradeForge ("the App") is an educational chart-pattern scanner and paper-trading assistant. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Device identifier** — a random UUID generated on device to associate paper portfolios and settings. It is not your name, email, or phone number.
- **Portfolio & settings** — risk limits, strategy toggles, and paper trade history needed to operate the service.
- **Tickers you scan** — sent to the backend only to fetch market data and detect patterns.

We do not collect government IDs, payment card numbers, or brokerage passwords in the paper-first product.

## 2. How We Use Information

- To run pattern scans and paper trades you request
- To enforce risk limits and autonomous paper trading cycles
- To operate and improve reliability of the App

We do not sell your data.

## 3. Third-Party Services

- Market data via Yahoo Finance (yfinance)
- Optional backend hosting for the API

## 4. Your Choices

You can reset your paper portfolio, pause trading, enable the kill switch, or uninstall the App at any time.

## 5. Not Financial Advice

TradeForge is educational. Paper results do not guarantee live trading results. All investing involves risk of loss.

## 6. Contact

Open an issue on the TradeForge GitHub repository.
